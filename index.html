Welcome to Pat's Salmon Cookies Store! With 5 locations around the world, now YOU can have fishy cookie deliciousness internationally. Pat started as a small time baker in his mom's basement. When one fatefull evening 7 years ago, Pat was working on a batch of delicious chocolate chip cookies for his grandparents, when in a fit of butterfingers a whole SALMON fell into the batch!!!

Being the enterprising and courageous man he was, Pat tried the cookies anyways, and a star was born!! Pat loved them Grandma loved them, (Grandpa hated them) and 74% of all taste testers asked rate them as 'palatable'.

Each cookie is made with chocolate chips, salmon, and a whole lotta love.
